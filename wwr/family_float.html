Family float Day

As rafting takes place, there are special days that families are encouraged to visit the rivers and after rafting, the family floats on cool waters as awhole and enjoy the calmness of the breeze.

pack

As a family you are encourage to each come with extra clothings to change in after the floating since you will be wet. A towel and any lotions or medications that you may personally need on the float adventure.

Dress

You will need to come in light clothing,shorts and t-shirts that are comfortable and certainable lotion.

Fitness level and Precaution

Under 15year old parent supervision required on addition to our highly trained personels who will accompany the family for safety. Water Confidence of family members is strongly encouraged.

Duration and epectations

The Trip will be 6km with a 2 hours at maximum on the river. Along the family float comes seeing of exciting creatures and sites as we travle too and from the family float area.
