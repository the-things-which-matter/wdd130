Our differences make us unique

                     Just like our fingures and each person in the world is different and unique in their own way, Magic also has different types and styles which include close-up magic, stage magic, table magic and others. All these hang or use one or a combination of the following; These ten categories include; Levitation, Vanish, Production, Transformation, Transposition, Penetration, Restoration, Escape, Teleportation and Prediction. There are two main ways to go about it, eaither you already know what you want forexample card magic and you decide to start with that directly or you don't know where to start from and start from anywhere and learn as much magic as possible and then decide what you want to choose and focus on. It is recommende that you focud on some type of magic because focus can bring the most out of you in that particular field. Although this may be encouranged, it is still okay if one can decide to become a generalized magician and can perform in diffrent areas. The categories are explained simply as follows; levitation is the act magic object fly in air, Vanishing is when when and object is made to disappear during the performance, Production is when an object id made to apear out of thin air, Transformation is where an object is changed into another, Transposition is when object the positions of the objects are interchanged, Penetration is when something is made to enter inside something else, Escape is when and object or a person is made to leave an otherwise impossible situation or place, Telepotation is when an object or person is made to move quickly from one place to another, Prediction is when you guess correctly what will happen way before it happens. As we begin, you will start by learning from what already exists and what other magicians know and from the foundations of other magicians who came before you. These will be the different skills created by them mixed with the different categories of magic mentioned above. But later when you are done with the basics and needed practice ,we will teach you how to set up your mind so that you are able to create your own magic tricks from scratch so that you can develope a routine using the simple formular i will teach you. When you create your own magic tricks, you will feel proud of yourself and those who will use your tricks will be proud that you invented them and all this is part of leaving your mark on the world. creatons will live on and will be used by many others for years even when you pass on and that will be a legacy lived abd left.
